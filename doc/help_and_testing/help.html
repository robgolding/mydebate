Help

General

What kind of questions should i ask?
Any questions about using the MyDebate website or rooms are acceptable and will be answered by one of our help team as soon as possible.
Please try to be specific with your questions so we can give you more accurate answers to your problems.

What kind of questions should not be asked?
- Questions which have already been answered on the FAQ's.
- Questions asking about the creation of MyDebate or other similar unrelated topics will not be answered.
- Questions that are not related to the website or its functionality.

Account

Registering

How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the register link and filling in your:
- Desired username
- Desired password
- E-mail address
- Etc.
A confirmation email will be then sent to your e-mail address with an activation link.

It wont let me use the username I entered
This is probably because the username has already been taken, please pick a different username.

I can't find the confirmation email
Check your junk mail folder as it might be in there, if you still cant find it, please contact us and we will be happy to help.

You can log into your account by clicking the login button on the top right of any page and entering your username and password.

Navigating the website

How do I search for a room?
To search for a room just type a keyword into the search field on the top right of any page and click search.

How do I get a list of all the current rooms?
You can get a list of all the rooms that are currently happening by clicking the room tab on any webpage.

How do I see a list of registered users?
To get a list of the current registered users just click the users tab on any webpage.

Chatroom

How do i join a room?
To join a room either go to the rooms page or search for a room and click on the desired rooms name.

It wont let me enter the room, it says "voting currently in progress, unable to join room"
This is because the room is currently in its voting stage, please wait and you will be connected when the voting is over.

How do I leave a room?
To leave a room simply click the "Leave conference" button at the bottom of the room page.

How do i create a room?
To create a room click the "New Conference" button on the top right-hand side of the Rooms page.

How do i send a message? To send a message type your message in the box below the chat and click the send button or press enter.

How do i vote?
Voting is done after a set interval, a box will pop up in the room where it will give your options for voting.
