About {{site.name}}

The name says it all! Come join the community by registering with us! If you have an opinion and you want to discuss it with the world, you are at the right place.

In here, you can:

  • Start your own debates
  • Discuss existing topics
  • Vote to be heard
  • Compare opinions with fellow users
  • See what the majority think of your debate and hopefully a conclusion would be made
It is easy to use and it is free. So sign up today and get your opinions heard!


Latest Updates

[10.3.2010] Search is working as of today.
Users can now use the search options bar/tab to look for related discussions and debates. It is easy to use and just type the keyword of the debate that you would like to be discussing with the others in the search box, or alternatively the search tab.

[09.3.2010] Voting counts are now stored
Votes are now counted and stored so that the creator of the debate can review the statistic.

[08.3.2010] Voting percentage
Votes are showed in a piechart! Giving it a clearer view of the percentage voted.

[07.3.2010] Debate room creator can decide to continue or close the debate
After the voting, the creator of the room can choose to continue the debate or close the debate.
